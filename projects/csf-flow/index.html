Measuring cerebral spinal fluid during sleep and wakefulness (CSF Flow Study)

Measuring cerebral spinal fluid during sleep and wakefulness

Primary Investigator: Kristine Wilckens, PhD; wilckenska@upmc.edu

CSF Flow aims to image the flow of cerebral spinal fluid during sleep and wakefulness in adults using a 7 Tesla MRI scanner and 7T-compatible physiological and polysomnographic measures.
